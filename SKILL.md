# Launchy Brand Illustration System

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Launchy
**Mascot:** Space Monkey Astronaut — a friendly chimp in a spacesuit, floating weightlessly and waving
**Concept:** Launch, exploration, adventure, accessibility — space travel made approachable and fun

## Visual Style Framework

### Rendering Technique
- **Medium:** Bold black ink / rubber stamp / linocut block-print style
- **Color palette:** Strict two-tone — solid black on warm cream/off-white (#F5F0E8 background, #1A1A1A ink)
- **No gradients, no halftones** — pure solid fills with natural stamp-texture grain
- **Slight texture/noise** on edges to simulate hand-pressed printing imperfections

### Composition Rules
- **Centered subject** with generous negative space (breathing room on all sides)
- **Floating/weightless pose** — character is never grounded, always mid-air
- **Motion indicators:** Small curved speed lines near feet/lower body to suggest launch/drift
- **Environmental particles:** Scattered small dots and circles around the character (stars, space debris) — 5-8 particles, asymmetrically placed
- **No background elements** — no planets, no ground, no borders. The character exists in open space

### Character Design Principles
- **Proportions:** Chibi/super-deformed — oversized head (1:2 head-to-body ratio), stubby limbs, rounded silhouette
- **Expression:** Always friendly — simple dot eyes, small curved smile, open/waving gesture
- **Helmet:** Large circular visor with thick rim, face clearly visible inside
- **Spacesuit details:** Chest control panel (2 small rectangles), segmented joints at knees/elbows, bulky gloves and boots
- **Jetpack/backpack:** Compact rounded pack on back with a small nozzle/tube
- **Silhouette test:** Character must be fully recognizable as a solid black shape

### Typography (if text is added)
- **Style:** Bold, rounded sans-serif — friendly and geometric
- **Weight:** Heavy/Black — matches the thick ink lines of the illustration
- **Case:** Lowercase preferred for approachability; title case for headers
- **No thin or serif fonts** — everything stays chunky and high-contrast

## Tone & Personality

| Attribute       | Description                                                |
|-----------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Adventurous** | Always exploring, launching, reaching outward               |
| **Approachable**| Rounded shapes, friendly face, waving gesture — never intimidating |
| **Playful**     | Chibi proportions, bouncy pose, lighthearted energy         |
| **Retro**       | Stamp/print texture evokes vintage posters and indie zines  |
| **Minimal**     | Two colors max, no clutter, no unnecessary detail           |
| **Confident**   | Bold lines, strong contrast — nothing tentative or wispy    |

## Do / Don't

### Do
- Keep the character in a weightless, dynamic pose
- Use thick, confident strokes — nothing thinner than ~3px at print scale
- Maintain the hand-printed texture/grain on edges
- Leave ample white space around the character
- Keep facial features simple (dots + curves)

### Don't
- Add color beyond black and cream
- Use gradients, shadows, or 3D effects
- Over-detail the suit — keep it iconic, not technical
- Ground the character on a surface
- Add speech bubbles, text overlays, or UI elements to the mascot itself
- Make the character look aggressive, sad, or neutral — always friendly and upbeat

## Application Guidelines

### As App Icon / Favicon
- Crop tight to character, maintain circular or rounded-square frame
- Character should fill ~70% of the frame
- Cream background, no border

### As Hero Illustration
- Character centered with full breathing room
- Can add subtitle text below, never overlapping the character
- Particle dots can extend further from character for larger compositions

### As Loading/Empty State
- Character can be shown in different poses (thumbs up, looking through telescope, sitting on rocket)
- Same style rules apply — black ink on cream, stamp texture, no gradients
