slide 4 · cta

final carousel rules

  • each slide commits: either copy leads (lots of text, minimal marks) or ink leads (big art/mark, sparse text). never both. the feed's rhythm comes from alternating.
  • cherry bomb one = logo only. never titles, never decoration. just the blaztly wordmark.
  • big numbers = caveat 700 in ink, with no. superscript prefix on tip slides. never orange.
  • titles = space grotesk 700. body = dm sans. accents (tags, cues, labels) = caveat.
  • orange lives in two places only: the highlighter (one phrase per slide) and the cta button.
  • slides/layouts/buttons stay straight. only ink-drawn marks (scribbles, arrows, circles) can be crooked — because they ARE ink and ink is imperfect.
  • paper grain via svg noise on every slide — subtle, keeps it from feeling digital.
