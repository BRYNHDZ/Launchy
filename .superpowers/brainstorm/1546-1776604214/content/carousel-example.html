carousel anatomy — the rules

  • cover (slide 1): big hook with a number if possible. numbers are lowercase or use cherry bomb. always has blaztly wordmark + "swipe →" cue in footer.
  • tips (slides 2–n): one idea per slide. orange number in cherry bomb. space grotesk title, dm sans body. body copy can use sentence case since legibility matters in long text on a small screen.
  • cta (last slide): orange-highlighted phrase in the lead line. soft-sell cta. optional mascot appearance. "your turn." style closers work well.
  • every slide: cream bg, ink type, 2px ink border (optional), 3–4 scattered stamp dots (mix black + 1 orange). blaztly wordmark in footer.
  • lowercase default applies — except body paragraphs on tip slides, where sentence case is ok if legibility demands it.
  • mascot: appears on cover + cta slides. stays two-tone (ink on cream). never on middle tip slides — those are pure info.
