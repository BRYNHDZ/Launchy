what changed in v6

  • whitespace ++ — slide padding bumped (2.4rem), body copy trimmed, fewer elements per slide. the eye gets to rest.
  • ink marks on every slide: a sparkle asterisk + squiggly underline (cover), scattered ink dots + a big arrow (tip 1), dashed ink circle around the asset + wavy squiggle (tip 2), sparkle + arrow pointing to the cta (cta slide).
  • ink marks can be crooked (-12°, 18°, 35°) because they ARE ink — imperfection is their point. layout/type/buttons still stay straight.
  • orange still lives in two places only — the highlighter, and the cta button. numbers, tags, footer cues, ink marks = all ink or neutrals.
