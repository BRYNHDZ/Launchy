final template rules

  • big numbers = caveat 700, with no. superscript prefix on tip slides. on the cover (a hook number, not a list number), skip the no. prefix.
  • cherry bomb one = logo only. never titles, never body, never decorative. just the blaztly wordmark.
  • titles = space grotesk 700. body = dm sans. accents (tags, footer cues, slide labels) = caveat.
  • slides are straight. the only element allowed to be crooked is the scribble arrow — because it's ink-drawn, and ink is imperfect by nature.
  • cta button is always orange — orange fill, cream text, ink drop-shadow offset.
  • orange highlighter on one phrase per slide. never more. picks the word to remember.
  • paper grain via svg noise on every slide — subtle, but keeps it from feeling digital.
