which direction?

both use the real brand art, both stay in-palette. variation a is softer, warmer, zine-y — easy to mass-produce, reads friendly in the feed. variation b is bolder, louder, hits harder in a thumb-scroll but every post competes with itself visually. you could also mix: use the poster style for hook/cover slides and zine for tip slides.
